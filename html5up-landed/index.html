Welcome

Welcome to Explorers Southwest, we are a three-season adventurous business which provides tours and events. We deliver popular tours through the Mountains of Durango, Colorado.

Here at Explorers Southwest we love adventure and being outdoors. We want our customers to experience different activities outside their comfort zone and explore new places within our State of Colorado. The services we have are Jeep Tours around the mountains, Day Trips around the Mesa Verde and Rental Equipment for your own adventures. Find Out More Info Below.

Guided Jeep Tours

This Tour will take place in the Mountains of Durango, Colorado.

These will take place within the month of May to Early October. In groups of 8, you will explore the Mountains seeing a variety of wildlife and beautiful waterfalls. Also, follow La Plata River to La Plata City which will lead to an old 1875 mining town for you to discover.
