Guided Jeep Tours

Our Most Popular Activity by Customers

Durango is known for their mountain terrain. Durango have beautiful Rocky Mountains which brings a unique experience. This town is famous for their railroads which leads to Silverton. Also, Durango is the most populated town within the South West of Colorado with 16,000. Through our activities you will explore the Mountains of Durango and this will bring a very wholesome experience to all our customers.

These will take place within the month of May to Early October. In groups of 8, you will explore the Mountains seeing a variety of wildlife and beautiful waterfalls. Also, follow La Plata River to La Plata City which will lead to an old 1875 mining town for you to discover.
